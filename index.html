About Me & Experience

Eramalla Shivani

I am E. Shivani, a passionate interior designer with a Bachelor's degree in Interior Design from JNAFAU. My enthusiasm for design began in my early teenage years, driving me to pursue and excel in this field. I believe that interior design is like storytelling with spaces—we don't just arrange furniture; we create environments that reflect the lifestyle, personality, and needs of the people who live there.

Specializing in residential villas, commercial offices, modular kitchens, and hospitality projects, I bridge the gap between architectural layouts and human comfort using AutoCAD, SketchUp, Enscape, and V-Ray.
